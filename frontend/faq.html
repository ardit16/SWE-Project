Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a rider?

You can sign up as a rider by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the top right and selecting "To ride". Follow the instructions to complete your registration.

How do I become a driver?

To become a driver, click on the "Sign Up" button and select "To drive". Fill in the necessary information and submit your application. We will review your details and get back to you.

What safety measures does Rreze have in place?

We prioritize safety by conducting background checks on all drivers, offering in-app safety features, and providing 24/7 support.

How do I make a payment?

Payments can be made through the app using a credit card or cash. Simply select your preferred payment method before booking a ride.

What if I need help during my ride?

If you need help during your ride, you can use the in-app emergency button to contact our support team or emergency services.

What should I do if I left something in the car?

If you left something in the car, contact our support team through the app or website. Provide details of the lost item and the ride, and we will assist you in recovering it.

How do I provide feedback about my ride?

You can provide feedback about your ride through the app after your trip has ended. Rate your driver and leave comments about your experience to help us improve our services.
